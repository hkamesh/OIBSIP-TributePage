Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, fondly remembered as the *People’s President*, was an inspiring scientist, visionary leader, and a true patriot. He played a key role in India’s missile and space programs, earning him the title *“Missile Man of India.”* His humble beginnings in Rameswaram to becoming the 11th President of India show his deep faith in hard work, education, and service.

Dr. Kalam believed in empowering youth through education and innovation. His life was dedicated to the dream of a developed India, and he continues to inspire generations to think big, act with purpose, and stay grounded in values.
